# Native Limits

The active product does not ship scoreboard teams, collision rules, name-tag
visibility controls, or ping objectives. Those features require a non-native
surface in the current codebase and must not be faked.

## Scale

The adapter may need to update every subject entry for every viewer. This is
acceptable for small and medium proxy networks up to 250 online players.

If larger networks are required, add caching or dirty-set planning before
expanding the active contract.
